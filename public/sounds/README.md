# Sound Files Directory

Place your sound effect files here. Recommended formats:
- MP3 (best browser compatibility)
- OGG (good quality, smaller size)
- WAV (highest quality, larger size)

## Required Sound Files

Create or download these sound files:

### Animal Sounds
- `feed.mp3` - Cheerful eating sound (0.3-0.5s)
- `animal-happy.mp3` - Playful chirp or giggle

### Plant Sounds
- `water.mp3` - Gentle water splash (0.3-0.5s)
- `grow.mp3` - Magical growth chime

### Weather Sounds
- `rain.mp3` - Soft rain patter (loop, 5s)
- `sun.mp3` - Bright, warm ping (0.3s)
- `weather.mp3` - Whoosh transition (0.5s)

### Achievement Sounds
- `badge.mp3` - Victory fanfare (1-2s)
- `discover.mp3` - Magical sparkle (0.5-0.7s)
- `celebration.mp3` - Full celebration (2-3s)

### UI Sounds
- `tap.mp3` - Soft button click (0.1-0.2s)
- `swoosh.mp3` - Page transition (0.3s)

### Ambient Sounds (Optional)
- `night.mp3` - Crickets and night ambiance (loop)
- `day.mp3` - Birds chirping (loop)
- `forest.mp3` - Nature background (loop)

## Sound Guidelines

### Duration
- UI sounds: 0.1-0.2 seconds
- Interaction sounds: 0.3-0.7 seconds
- Celebration sounds: 1-3 seconds
- Ambient loops: 5-10 seconds

### Volume
- All sounds should be normalized
- Peak at around -6dB to -3dB
- No clipping or distortion

### Quality
- Sample rate: 44.1kHz or 48kHz
- Bit depth: 16-bit minimum
- Stereo or mono (mono preferred for smaller size)

### Child-Friendly Requirements
- No sudden loud noises
- No scary sounds
- Pleasant, friendly tones
- Clear, crisp quality
